About TED

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world.

About TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. TEDx is an international community that organizes TED-style events anywhere and everywhere, commemorating locally-driven ideas and elevating them to a global stage. TEDx events are produced independently of TED conferences, each event curates speakers on their own, but according to TED’s format and rules.

About KIET Group Of Institutions

KIET Group of Institutions is a premier technical institute in the NCR region. To lay a strong foundation in the technical education domain the members of the Krishna Charitable Society initiated the KIET Group of Institutions in 1998 with a modest number of 180 students that have reached a humongous number of 5600 + students on campus today, towards this journey of excellence in the technical domain. The institute has gained a distinct image as an outstanding educational colossal among technical institutions of Uttar Pradesh, due to its inclination towards innovative and skill-based education.

About TEDxKIET

TEDx KIET is an independently organized TED event focused on bringing together, speakers and participants under one roof to strike up conversations that matter. Following the dynamics of TED talks, TEDxKIET aims to bring the same format for the students of KIET Group of Institutions. TEDx talks are a great way to improve general knowledge. They not only enlighten students about any specific field but also enriches them with the gist from a huge diversity of subjects, directly from the insiders. The club TEDxKIET was established in March 2018, with the sole motive of stimulating the interest of students to the zestful domain of TED talks. The first edition of TEDxKIET revolved around the theme “The World is our Playground” which witnessed one of the most exceptional experiences in the history of KIET. Since then this club works to enlighten the students by bringing the revolutionary talks
