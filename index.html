ABOUT

In the spirit of “ideas worth spreading,” TED created TEDx, a program of local, self-organised, independent events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, a collection of TED Talk videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussions and connections.

Past Events

TEDxKIET 2021 events

The organising team successfully hosted two physicals events in last year 2021, TED Circles- Sept. 2021 and TEDxKIET-2021, and put together a revolutionary line-up of speakers consisting of artists, celebrities, industrialists, visionaries and motivators who inspired thousands of people with their words of change.
